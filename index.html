Our Heritage

From the lanes of Rudhauli Bazaar, since 1962.

Three generations ago, our grandfather opened a small sarrafa shop on a quiet lane in Rudhauli, Basti. Today his grandsons continue the same promise — every gram weighed in front of you, every hallmark verified, every piece backed by a name the district has trusted for sixty years.

We don't follow trends. We make jewellery the way mothers want for their daughters — meant to be worn for decades, then passed on.

Om Nath Sarraf — Founder, 1962
